# Influence of Financial Education on Personal Budgeting among University Students in Ecuador

## Research Project

This repository contains the academic work developed for the course **Artificial Intelligence in Research**.

### Research Topic

**Influence of Financial Education on Personal Budgeting among University Students in Ecuador.**

### Objectives

- Review the scientific literature.
- Identify research gaps.
- Translate the literature review into academic English.
- Identify potential journals for publication.
- Apply Artificial Intelligence tools ethically throughout the research process.

### Artificial Intelligence Tools Used

- Consensus
- Elicit
- NotebookLM
- ChatGPT
- DeepL
- Grammarly

### Repository Contents

- Literature Review (Spanish)
- Literature Review (English)
- Journal Mapping
- Literature Matrix
- Prompts used during the research process
